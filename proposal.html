Diagnosis

What I see from the outside.
Five opportunities.

You can't buy your way out of this with more Google Ads. It's operations, brand, and discipline. Any consultant spots these leaks in a week. The value is closing them in thirty days.

01

Generic positioning

The site's H1 says "Austin Catering for All Occasions". MML Catering, Sterling Events, Crave, or austincatering.com could sign that line without changing a word.
→ Rewrite hero in week 1
02

Aligned investment management

We see high competition on Google Ads. Since this investment is balanced for Contigo, it would be worth understanding how it integrates with Meta spend and balancing brand / performance.
→ Reallocation, not more spend
03

Quote pricing management

It's important to measure response time from the moment the quote request arrives, and we understand that the granular nature of orders — for example whether "the limes are in season" or not — are things to address. This is a market matter: some already have it hyper-solved. I want to live the Contigo experience.
→ Internal pricing matrix, < 4h response
04

One Eleven East opportunity

As a public venue it competes with 200 event spaces. As the ecosystem's own theater, it competes with nobody — no one else has a controlled venue to host their own catering with their own tableware.
→ Reactivate as Tasting Engine
05

Four brands run as four separate businesses

Contigo, Ocaso, Table Manners, One Eleven East — each with its own site, its own pipeline, its own identity. Right now they're burning the synergy that justified building all four. As an integrated ecosystem, each account can activate 2–4 verticals and lift ticket size 30–50%.
→ Reframe: 1 system, 4 surfaces

The framework

What makes this proposal
incomparable.

Five principles applied to Contigo's operational reality. Not motivational quotes — decisions that change who you take on and who you turn away.

01

Specificity is the new luxury

AI made generic cheap. Any agency delivers "Austin catering strategy" in 20 minutes. What can't be automated is the judgment of what to say no to.
02

Smallest viable market

Not "win Austin." Win 40 specific offices across 5 corridors. Every account has a name, address, decision-maker by title, and a closing horizon.
03

Permission, not interruption

ezCater is rented interruption — you pay 20% of the ticket forever. The real asset is a house list of Office Managers who open an email every Monday.
04

Status and belonging

Bumble's Office Manager isn't buying catering — they're buying a signal about the kind of company Bumble is. Contigo has to be the brand people choose to mean something.
05

Let's be the Purple cow

Commit to ONE public, verifiable thing no one else publishes. My candidate: farm-named sourcing + visible chef-founder + a transparent pricing matrix for enterprise (not public).
06

The Tasting Engine

The synthesis: one unique, measurable NSM. # of tastings closed and converted. Everything else is subordinate to this.

"We're going to build the caterer a Fortune 500 chooses to signal who they are,
not to save two dollars per person."
